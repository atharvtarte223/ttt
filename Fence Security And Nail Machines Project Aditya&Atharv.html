TECHNICAL DESCRIPTION-

Chain Link Machine for manufacturing Chain Link Fence of different size . The wire is taken in form the payoff stand and passed through the Dies where the diamond size take place. Different gap sized. After Passing from the die the diamond pattern wire is automatically throw in the winding shaft and automatic weaving in Chain Link mesh as per your specific length. After weaving machine will cut that wire automatically.
